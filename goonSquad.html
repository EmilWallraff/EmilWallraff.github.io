Goon Squad

Multiplayer First-Person-Shooter
Unreal Engine

Goon Squad is a multiplayer first-person shooter featuring various characters. These characters distinguish themselves through their unique playstyles, designs, and personalities. The game is set in a vibrant, comic-inspired universe and is currently under development by me in Unreal Engine.

All characters are equipped with different weapons and possess a unique set of abilities that define their distinct playstyles. These abilities and weapons within a character's kit are often designed to synergize with each other. Furthermore, every character has a fully developed personality and backstory, which is hinted at through their models, voice lines, and character art.

Goon Squad offers more than just precise and fast-paced multiplayer gameplay; it also places a significant emphasis on the game's visual style. A fusion of the character and weapon models I designed, along with the environments, shaders, and materials, ensures that the game not only maintains readability but also boasts a distinctive aesthetic reminiscent of comic books.
