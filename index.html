Plant Generator

Plant Generator is a Unity Plugin developed by Jan Ruppert and me in cooperation with CritCrew. Its purpose is to create and grow 3D models of plants directly in Unity. Additionally, the Plant Generator can combine features of existing plants to create new ones.

Verlorene Wege

Verlorene Wege (English: Lost Ways) is an interactive story experience that combines both game sequences and live-action film. It was developed by me and other students as part of a collaboration between my university and the THWS Faculty of Design. The story revolves around a young man named Pierre, who is compelled to confront his father and grapple with his own toxic masculinity.
