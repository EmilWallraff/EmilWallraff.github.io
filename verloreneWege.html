Verlorene Wege (English: Lost Ways) is an interactive story experience that combines both game sequences and live-action film. It was developed by me and other students as part of a collaboration between my university and the THWS Faculty of Design. The story revolves around a young man named Pierre, who is compelled to confront his father and grapple with his own toxic masculinity.

Verlorene Wege was a highly collaborative project, emphasizing communication, rapid prototyping, and adjustments to realize a complex vision for a multifaceted story. This approach resulted in a challenging yet ultimately rewarding development process creating five distinct and mostly independent game sequences, each contributing to different aspects of the overarching narrative.

In addition to the software development process and story creation, this project also included the recording of live-action footage, which was used for both the film sequences and the rotoscope animation-based graphics featured in the game sequences.
